About Party World

Party World is a party supplies retail shop based at Star Mall, Kamkunji, Nairobi, specialising in balloons, stickers, candles, decorations, and party accessories. The business has been operational since April 2025 and primarily serves walk-in customers.

Stock is sourced through direct imports from ChinaLand and Kaboww. The shop runs on Odoo 18 for full business management including sales, inventory, and accounting.
